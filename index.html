About Prodigy

Engage, Express and Evolve this year with Prodigy’20! Its here to take you on an intellectual expedition. Away from the mundane activities, explore a thrilling new world of science, technology and innovations. With a plethora of mind boggling events and workshops, it aims to bring together young, ignited minds and awaken and sharpen their inner talents. Come, let’s explore the unexplored with Prodigy’20!
